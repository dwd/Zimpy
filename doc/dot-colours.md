# Presence Dot Colors

Mapping of presence states to the dot colors used in the contacts list.

- No presence received (unknown/offline): `#B0B7BF`
- `PresenceShowElement.CHAT` (online/available): `#2FB84D`
- `PresenceShowElement.AWAY` (away): `#F9A825`
- `PresenceShowElement.DND` (do not disturb): `#C62828`
- `PresenceShowElement.XA` (extended away/unavailable): `#F9A825`
- Presence status `Unavailable` (from XEP-0084 mapping in xmpp_stone): treated as offline (`#B0B7BF`)
